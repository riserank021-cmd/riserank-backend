FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about RiseRank.

Is RiseRank completely free? +
Yes, RiseRank is 100% free. There is no subscription, no premium tier and no hidden fees. All features including daily quizzes, current affairs, All India Rank, leaderboard and bookmarks are available at zero cost.
Which exams does RiseRank cover? +
RiseRank currently covers SSC (CGL, CHSL, MTS, GD, CPO), Railway (NTPC, Group D, JE, ALP), Banking (IBPS PO, Clerk, SBI PO, Clerk, RRB) and Bihar SI / Police exams. More exams are being added continuously in 2026.
How does the All India Rank work? +
After you submit any quiz, RiseRank instantly calculates your rank among all students who attempted the same quiz. For example, "Rank 247 / 8,420 students" means you scored better than 8,173 students. Your rank updates in real time as more students attempt the quiz.
Is the content available in Hindi? +
Yes! All quiz questions and current affairs are available in both English and Hindi. You can switch between languages with a single tap at any time during the quiz.
How does the Daily Quiz work? +
A fresh quiz is published every day. Each quiz has MCQ questions tailored to your exam category. You get detailed explanations after submitting, and your rank is reflected in the leaderboard immediately.
Can I practice offline? +
Yes. RiseRank caches quiz content automatically when you are connected to the internet. You can attempt cached quizzes without any internet connection. Your score and rank sync when you go back online.
Is RiseRank available on iOS? +
Currently RiseRank is available on Android (Play Store). iOS support is in development and will be released in 2026. You can register your interest via email and we will notify you when it launches.
