Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

RiseRank ("we", "us", or "our") operates the RiseRank mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you register for an account, we collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Password (stored as a secure hash — never in plain text)
  • Preferred exam categories

Usage Data

We automatically collect information about how you use the App, including:

  • Quiz attempts, scores, and performance history
  • Current affairs articles viewed
  • Live test participation
  • Leaderboard rankings
  • App feature interactions

Device Information

We may collect device-specific information such as your device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's core functionality
  • Track your exam preparation progress and scores
  • Generate personalised quiz recommendations
  • Display leaderboard rankings and All India Rank
  • Send push notifications about daily quizzes and current affairs (if enabled)
  • Analyse and improve App performance and features
  • Deliver relevant advertisements through Google AdMob

3. Advertising

The RiseRank App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may use your device's Advertising ID and other information to serve personalised ads based on your interests.

You can opt out of personalised advertising at any time by adjusting your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation

For more information on how Google uses data from apps that use its services, visit: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Google AdMob — for ad delivery and measurement
  • Firebase — for push notifications and analytics
  • Service providers — who assist us in operating the App under confidentiality obligations
  • Legal authorities — if required by law or to protect our rights

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. Quiz history and performance data are retained to provide you with progress tracking. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS), hashed password storage, and access controls. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Children's Privacy

The RiseRank App is intended for users aged 18 and above and is designed for competitive exam preparation (SSC, Railway, Banking, Bihar SI, etc.). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt out of personalised advertising
  • Disable push notifications via device settings

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@riserank.in.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: support@riserank.in
  • Website: https://riserank.in
